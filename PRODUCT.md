# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who frequently compare crypto exchange C2C/P2P prices for CNY stablecoin on-ramp and off-ramp decisions. They use the app in short glances from the macOS menu bar while doing other work.

## Product Purpose

C2CBar shows the best available C2C prices for USDT and USDC across exchanges, with quick comparison against a reference USD/CNY rate. Success means the user can see the current best buy/sell prices, the source exchange, and premium at a glance without opening exchange websites.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, trader-focused.

## Anti-references

Avoid landing-page styling, oversized controls, decorative cards, empty hero layouts, or generic crypto-dashboard spectacle. The interface should not feel like a web page squeezed into a menu bar.

## Design Principles

- Lead with prices, not explanation.
- Keep the menu bar panel compact and scan-first.
- Make exchange/source confidence visible without visual noise.
- Preserve room for dense repeated use.
- Treat mock data and stale data states clearly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use legible contrast in dark mode, stable fixed dimensions to avoid layout jump, and text labels alongside color-coded price states where meaning matters.
